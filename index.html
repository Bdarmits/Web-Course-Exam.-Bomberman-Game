How to play

✖

Use the arrow keys to move on the board

Press 'B' to place a bomb. Bomb will explode after 3 seconds of delay.

Game has 10 levels.

To win you shoul destroy all zombies and find the key, to open the door.

To drop level use button in the right-top corner of the screen.

Items Description

✖

Rose: Instantly kills one Zombie on the map. Drop Chance: ~1/80 Duration: permanent

Apple: Removes all trees from the the map. Drop Chance: ~1/80 Duration: permanent

Bomb: Enlarges bomb blast radius. Drop Chance: ~3/80 Duration: 5sec<
